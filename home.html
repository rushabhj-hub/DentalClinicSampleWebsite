Providing Free Dental Check Ups Now!

As per statistics, the average number of time a person in urban India visits a dentist less than 3 times in a life time. More than 90% of Indians suffer from caries. It is recommended that a person should visit a dentist every 6 months for a dental check-up. Also, most dentists advise a professional dental scaling and cleaning at a dental clinic every 6 months. At The City Dental Clinic, we believe we can change the oral habits of the Indian population by making dental checkups easy & accessible. One of the biggest barriers to a dental checkup from the point of view of the patient is the cost. Hence, we believe that the best way to promote the habit of 6 monthly dental checkups is by providing them Free of Charge. Hence, we offer Free Checkups. How can I protect my Dental Health ?

  • Teeth Brushing at least twice a day
  • Healthy food and timely regulated meal/snacks
  • Replacing toothbrush after at least three to four months
  • Maintaining oral care of your children with guided teeth cleaning techniques
  • Immediate dental consultation on experiencing any of dental issues to avoid further worsening
  • Regular flossing
  • Pregnant women suggested to eat healthy and stay orally fit
  • Regular oral checkups
  • Smoking cessation. Smoke badly damage oral health. Have regular dental checkups if smoking regularly
