Special Care We Offer

1.Easy to Care For – You don’t need to do anything special to care for your dental implant. Just brush and floss normally, and visit your dentist regularly for teeth cleanings and examinations. That’s it!
2.Long-Lasting – Dental implants are a great investment, usually lasting twice as long as other restorations. With proper oral hygiene, dental implants can last for the rest of your life.
3.Looks & Feels Natural – Dental implants are custom-made to match the appearance and function of your natural teeth. You’ll be able to eat and speak naturally and won’t feel self-conscious about your smile.
4.Designed For Your Health – Titanium and zirconium implants encourage the growth of healthy bone and gum tissue.
5.Prevent bone loss and tooth movement -. When a tooth is removed, the bone that used to support that tooth will resorb over time, creating defects or dents, changes in face shape, and collapse of the cheeks and lips to make the patient appear older than they are. Implants stimulate and support, maintain density, and of bone just as the bone surrounding natural teeth does.
6.Prevent teeth Movement – Also, our teeth prefer to have partners: if a tooth is removed, the opposing tooth will often grow out of the bone because there is no contact. Teeth will also tip or move forward into areas where teeth are missing which can alter and disrupt proper biting. Implants maintain the space and prevent any teeth movement.
